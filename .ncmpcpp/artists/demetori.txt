Demetori is a doujin group that produces mostly instrumental hard rock and metal arrangements of Touhou music.

Members

寺前甲 (Masaru Taramae) a.k.a 徳南 (Tokunan) - Guitars, Bass, Keyboards, Woodwind, Additional instruments
寺前直 (Tadashi Teramae) a.k.a 九宝時 (Kyuhouji) - Drums, Percussions

Masaru Teramae is also a current member of the Falcom jdk Band.
